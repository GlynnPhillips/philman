Stay with us!

We are really excited to be getting married this summer and want our closest friends and family to enjoy the wedding as much as we will. Which is why we would like to invite you to stay with us at Tresillian House.

Tresillian House is a beautiful country manor house built in 1849. The house is set within 22 acres of lawns, orchards and a victorian walled garden producing flowers and vegetables for the house. We will have private access to the property and grounds for the whole weekend from Friday 12th until Monday 15th July.

The rooms

Alongside the main house, there is also a converted apple store and a farm house. Between the three buildings we have enough room for our close friends and family to stay for the whole weekend.

Most of the rooms are large double bedrooms, with a couple of big twin rooms, and most have ensuite bathrooms. The three buildings also have their own shared kitchens and living areas, as well as larger shared bathrooms.

You will be able to use the rooms from Friday afternoon (exact time TBC) until 10am on Monday morning, but you can arrive and leave at any time that suits you.
