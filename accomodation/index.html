Stay with us!

We are really excited to be getting married and want our close friends and family to enjoy the wedding as much as we will. Which is why we would like to invite you to stay with us at Tresillian House.

Tresillian house is Regency period country house originally built in 1849. The house is set within 22 acres of lawns, orchards and a victorian walled garden producing flowers and vegetables for the house.

We will have private access to the property and grounds for the whole weekend from Friday 12th until Monday 15th July.

The rooms

Along side the main house there is also a converterd apple store and farm house each with their own kitchen and living room. This means we hope to have a good group of people staying and enjoying the grounds for the weekend.

Most of the rooms are large doubles with a couple of big twins. Many of the rooms also have their own ensuites.

You will be able to use the rooms from Friday afternoon (exact time tbc) until 10am on Monday morning, but you can arrive and leave at any time that suits you.
