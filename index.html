Places to stay

The area is a popular holiday destination and therefore the venue is surrounded by holiday homes, hotels & campsites. We have included some of our favourites but you are welcome to stay anywhere that suits you.

The plume of feathers

A charming 16th century inn turned luxury B&B country pub that is less than 10 minutes from our venue by taxi. More info for The Plume of Feathers

Stable Conversion (AirBnB)

A stunning 2 bedroom stable conversion with 2 double beds and a single. The stable is 15 minutes from our venue by taxi. More info for The Stable

The Granary (AirBnB)

The Granary is a converted barn that sleeps 4 in a double room and a twin room. The Granary is less than 10 minutes from our venue by taxi. More info for The Granary

Carvynick Holiday Park

Carvynick has 30 self-catering holiday cottages along with campervan and tent pitches. There is also the fantastic The Foxs Revenge pub on site that serves breakfast, lunch and dinner. More info for Carvynick

Premier Inn

There are 2 Premier Inn's close to the venue, Quintrell Downs is less than 5 minutes and Fraddon is less than 10 minutes from our venue by taxi. More info for Premier Inn Quintrell Downs or More info for Premier Inn Fraddon
