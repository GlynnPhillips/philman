The big day

Where

We are really excited to be getting married at Tresillian House in Cornwall. Tresillian House is a beautiful country manor house built in 1849. The house is set within 22 acres of lawns, orchards and a Victorian walled garden producing flowers and vegetables for the house.

Tresillian House
Summercourt
Cornwall
TR8 4PS

When

The ceremony will begin at one o’clock so please arrive for 12:30pm. Unfortunately the dancing will have to end at around midnight, so taxis will need to be arranged for 12:30am.

Dress code

Please wear whatever makes you happy! Fingers crossed the weather will be nice and we’ll be able to enjoy the gardens. If you want to bring spare shoes then there will be bag storage.

Drinks

Later in the evening there will be a cash bar, so please make sure you bring a little bit of cash.

Gifts

We are so excited to spend the day celebrating with our closest friends and our families, and we’d rather you spent the money on travel or somewhere to stay. But if you are still keen to give us a gift, you can contribute to towards experiences on our honeymoon via our Prezola list

Transport

If you are arriving by public transport, feel free to let us know your arrival time and we will do our best to help arrange a lift with someone.

By Car

The wedding venue is just a couple of minutes drive off the A30, along the A3058 near Summercourt in Cornwall.

By Train

The nearest train station is Quintrell Downs, which is on the Atlantic Coast line running between Par and Newquay. Trains from London Paddington take around 5 hours including a change at Par, or alternatively you can take a 20 minute taxi from St. Austell station.

To get the best deal on train tickets, we suggest setting a reminder for around the 20th April 2019, as tickets normally go on sale around 12 weeks in advance.

By Plane

The venue is only a short drive from Newquay airport, which usually has 3 flights per day to and from London Heathrow airport. Flight time is around 1 hour.

Local Taxis

If you are planning to stay nearby and travel to or from our wedding venue by taxi, then we can recommend Newquay Taxis (0163 741 8900 ), 10-11 Taxis (0163 787 1011), and A2B Newquay (0163 787 7777).

Cabs home at the end of the night should be booked for half past twelve, please.
